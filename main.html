Aurinko API benefits

Increase value of your apps

Since Aurinko API is embeddable, users can access any and all of their needed services inside of your application without ever knowing that Aurinko is there at all, increasing your customers’ engagement, productivity and retention rates.

Event-based sync

Event-based sync is central to applications’ use of Aurinko API. Keeping track of events that occur in your end user’s cloud service is pivotal to providing your users with the information they need from your application. Aurinko provides this data through our API by utilizing webhooks technology and data mapping.

Frictionless security

Protecting your sensitive data is a top priority. That is why we developed our API as a pass-though that never stores your data on our servers and have advanced security controls that have built-in OAuth2. In addition, your data is encrypted and isolated with multi-level permission checks. We give you full control over what data is passed through Aurinko API so your customer’s data is always secure.

Save development time

Why waste years of development time when you can integrate your app with Google, Outlook, Office 365 and Exchange services in just days? Aurinko API has all pre-built features that can sync your app with email, calendar and contact services with just a few lines of code. In addition, Aurinko comes with pre-built security features that provide a secure and reliable connection that needs to be configured only once. We handle ongoing maintenance and updates for you.

BrightSync — pre-build sync logic

Aurinko has built a configurable BrightSync engine for CRM, project management, human resources, accounting, marketing and other platform providers that migrates data between their platform and mailboxes automatically.

Contact us to learn more about BrightSync and avoid re-inventing and developing the sync logic.

BrightSync is capable of:

  • Two way calendar sync between user mailbox and the business system
  • One way email logging to the business system with contact/opportunity matching
  • Two way contacts sync between user mailbox and the business system
  • Two way tasks sync between user mailbox and the business system

Inbox plugin — data at your users fingertips

Aurinko has built a highly customizable inbox plugin for CRM, project management, human resources, accounting, marketing and other platform providers that would like their users to access their platforms from within their inboxes.

Contact us to learn more about our inbox plugin.

The plugin is capable of:

  • Instantly showing platform data in users’ inboxes. Aurinko inbox app surfaces relevant data based on emails, contacts, tasks and/or meeting fields.
  • Let your users quickly create, read, update and delete calendar invites, tasks, leads and contacts right from their inboxes.
  • Simplifying activity capture — allow your users to easily log calendar events, tasks, contacts, leads, emails and attachments to your platform and associate them with the right opportunities, contacts and/or leads.
  • Automating follow-up process — users can prepare follow-up sequences once and let API logic send follow-up emails for them. Aurinko monitors email threads and sends follow-up emails automatically if no response is received.
